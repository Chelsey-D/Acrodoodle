How to play:

Every day a new 4-letter acronym will be generated. Users think of what they want the acronym to stand for, then draw it using our doodleboard. Users submit their doodles it to see how many votes and favorites their doodle gets from other users. At the end of the day (11:59 pm Mountain Standard Time), the doodle with the most votes will be displayed for 24 hours and the user will enjoy the honored privilege of 'Supreme Doodler'* until they are dethroned by a new doodler.

*No special privileges apply.

How ACROdoodle came to be...

ACROdoodle was a game to pass the time during church. One of us would come up with an acronym and the other would draw what they thought the acronym stood for. Very creative people like us have a lot of fun with this game. We hope you do too!
